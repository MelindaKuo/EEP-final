Our Mission

Enriching lives through teaching English

Who Are We

We are a group of high school student volunteers in the US who are committed to bringing English learning opportunities to Taiwanese elementary school students.

What We Do

We strive to provide useful and fun English lessons to students who are beginning to learn English. Our weekly one-on-one online sessions focus on real-life conversations and aspects of American culture. Students are encouraged to practice and speak English with our friendly volunteer tutors.
